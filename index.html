How to Login to Your Uphold Account on a Desktop?

If You Already Have an Uphold Account: 1) Visit the Uphold website and select the "Log In" button located in the upper right corner. 2) In the spaces provided, enter your password and email address. 3) Select the "Log In" option. Your Uphold account should now be visible to you.

How to Access Your Uphold Account on a Mobile?

Device If you have already installed the Uphold app on your mobile device, the following steps will help you access your account: 1. Select "Log In" when the app is opened. 2. Tap "Continue" after entering the Uphold account's email address. 3. Look in your inbox for an Uphold email with the subject line "Action Required:." Please verify your email address. 4. In the email, tap the "Confirm Email Address" button. 5. When you return to the Uphold app, you will be prompted to enter your password. 6. After entering your password, tap "Log In." On your mobile device, you are now logged in to your Uphold account!

How to Login to Your Uphold Account on a Tablet?

If You Already Have the Uphold App Downloaded: 1. Open the app and go to the main screen and select "Log In." 2. Enter your password and registered email address. 3. To continue, tap "Log In." 4. You will be asked to enter a 6-digit code if your account supports Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). The phone number or email address associated with your 2FA settings will receive this code. 5. To access your account, tap "Log In" after entering the code.
